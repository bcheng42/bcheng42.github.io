2. Dashboard Redesign

During the summer of 2011, Magoosh added video lessons to our GRE test preparation product. In order to integrate these changes, I was tasked with conducting user tests to discover the best way to integrate lessons (and lesson suggestions) into the product dashboard. After eleven hours of user testing, I learned:

  1. Users heavily use the two green buttons to start practice sessions.
  2. Users wanted to see a visual depiction of their progress, and especially liked pie charts.
  3. There was a disconnect between the parts of the dashboard and the rest of the product.
As a result, we made the following design decisions:
  1. Keep the green quick start buttons so users can continue to quickly start practice sessions.
  2. More clear visualization of user's study progress.
  3. Connect the parts of the dashboard to related product pages with small corner links.

  3. There was a disconnect between the parts of the dashboard and the rest of the product.

The New Dashboard: Incorporates the changes from previous iterations, derived from user feedback. The new dashboard features:

  1. Suggested Lessons: An easy way for users to find relevant video lessons and follow a video curriculum.
  2. Progress Charts: A visual way for users to track their own progress and compare with other users.
  3. Improved Navigation: The new dashboard features links that allow users to easily navigate to the various sections of Magoosh GRE Prep.
