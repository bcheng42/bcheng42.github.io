Program Manager Internship

June - August 2013
Redmond, WA


Overview

I spent a twelve-week summer internship at Microsoft as a Program Manager Intern on the Apps for Creative Expression team, working on Movie Moments, a Windows 8.1 Modern App.

  • Collaborated with designers and program managers to design and create functional specs for new styles (themes for users to customize and caption their videos)
  • Worked with development and test teams to implement and test styles over the course of my internship, planning implementation in two-week sprints
  • Collaborated across teams to design and prototype a new mobile application: Created mockups using PowerPoint Storyboard and solicited feedback across mobile teams, then used XAML/C# to implement a front-end prototype to demonstrate the user experience

Preparation

To prepare for this role, I read the following books and articles:

  • The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman
  • The Mythical Man-Month by Frederick Brooks
  • The Elements of Typographic Style by Robert Bringhurst
  • PM at Microsoft by Steven Sinofsky
  • Zen of PM

Projects

  1. Design, spec, and implement two new sets of styles for Movie Moments
  2. Design and prototype the front-end user experience for a new mobile application

What I Learned

  • The role of the PM as product owner and bridge between designers, developers, and testers
  • How to create a functional specification for a feature and communicate the spec with my team
  • The considerations involved in making decisions within a large organization
  • Importance of communication and proactiveness within and across teams
  • Value of front-end prototyping in interaction design
  • Basic XAML/C# for prototyping Windows Phone applications
